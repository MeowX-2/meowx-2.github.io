About Me

Hello! I’m Arifa, currently pursuing a double major in Mathematics and Computer Science at Brac University.

I have a strong background in mathematical problem-solving and competitions. I represented Bangladesh at the European Girls’ Mathematical Olympiad (EGMO) from 2021 to 2023, where I earned a Bronze Medal. From 2018 to 2023, I was a consistent participant in the Bangladesh Mathematical Olympiad (BDMO), and later contributed as an Academic Team Member for both BDMO and the Bangladesh Girls’ Math Foundation (BGMF).

Alongside mathematics, I am passionate about computer science and programming. I was a National Qualifier at the Bangladesh Informatics Olympiad (BDOI) in 2023, and my interests now include frontend development with React and Next.js, Linux systems, and workflow optimization.

Outside of academics and coding, I enjoy chess, reading, writing, digital art, and sewing with my grandmother.

For a more detailed overview of my academic background, achievements, and experiences, you can connect with me on LinkedIn or view my CV, alongside my presence on Mail, Duolingo, Chess.com, Github, Spotify, and Substack.
