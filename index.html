About Me

I am Arifa, a Mathematics and Computer Science double major at Brac University. My academic journey is defined by a rigorous pursuit of problem-solving, highlighted by representing Bangladesh at the European Girls’ Mathematical Olympiad (EGMO) for three consecutive years (2021–2023) and earning a Bronze Medal.

My foundation in competitive mathematics runs deep. I was a consistent participant and later an Academic Team Member for both the Bangladesh Mathematical Olympiad (BDMO) and the Bangladesh Girls’ Math Foundation (BGMF). Bridging math with computing, I also qualified for the National Round of the Bangladesh Informatics Olympiad (BDOI) in 2023.

Currently, I am channeling this analytical background into software development. I specialize in building efficient, modern web applications using React and Next.js, with a keen interest in Linux systems and workflow optimization.

When I’m not coding or solving equations, I explore the creative intersection of logic and art—whether through chess, digital art, or traditional sewing with my grandmother.
